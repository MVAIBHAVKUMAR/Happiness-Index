Did You Know?

  • There is a misconception that self-acceptance will cause laziness, complacency and lack of drive.

  • In reality the research shows us that those who develop self acceptance and learn to be self-compassionate are less likely to fear failure and more likely to try again when they do fail.

  • Self-acceptance is not the same as passively accepting defeat.

  • Self-compassion often involves taking the more difficult road that is in your best interests.
